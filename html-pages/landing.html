How it works

Three steps. No account needed.

1

Pick your gym

See a live count of how many students are currently checked in at CRWC, Field House, and Fitness East.

2

Check in when you arrive

Tap "Check In" the moment you walk through the door. Your count is added to the live total.

3

Check out when you leave

Tap "Check Out" on your way out. The count drops in real time for everyone watching.
